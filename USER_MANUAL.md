# User Manual - Rythu Sethu

Welcome to **Rythu Sethu** ("Farmer's Bridge"), a personalized smart agricultural guide for Telangana farmers. This manual describes how to use all the features of this application to plan crops, check government scheme eligibility, find nearby support centers, read official government circulars, calculate fertilizer requirements, check market prices, and view localized weather advice.

---

## 🌍 Getting Started

### 1. Language Selection
At the top right corner of the dashboard, you will find three language circles:
*   **English**: Default English terminology.
*   **తెలుగు (Telugu)**: Native regional script. Highly readable layout.
*   **اردو (Urdu)**: High-legibility Urdu script rendering.

Tap any of the languages to dynamically translate all buttons, lists, forms, and advice blocks instantly.

---

## 💬 Feature 1: Smart Agri Chatbot
Ask questions about crops, soil health, pest control, organic manure, weeding, and subsidies in your preferred language.

*   **Ask via Text**: Type your agricultural query into the input box at the bottom of the screen and press the green Send button.
*   **Quick Prompts**: Above the input box, tap on key suggestion capsules (e.g., "Paddy Pest Control", "Drip Irrigation Subsidy") to quickly send common questions.
*   **Speakout (Audio Read Aloud)**:
    *   To hear the chatbot speak out, ensure the Volume icon at the top of the chatbot module is turned ON (`Active`).
    *   Each message from the AI advisor includes a distinct **Volume2 (Speaker 🔊)** button. Click this button to hear the diagnostic advice read aloud in your native language (Telugu or Urdu/English).
*   **Text Adjustment**: Click the `AA` Text Size toggle to dynamically scale the font size to your visual comfort (supports Small, Medium, Large, and Extra Large sizes).
*   **Copy Text**: Click the Paper Document copy button on any advice to copy it to your clipping clipboard.

---

## 📋 Feature 2: Scheme Eligibility Check (Quiz)
Verify whether you are eligible for prime Telangana state government agricultural programs in seconds.

1.  Select the desired scheme from the grid (e.g., **Rythu Bandhu Investment Support**, **Telangana Micro Irrigation Subsidies**, **PM Kisan Samman Nidhi**, or **Crop Insurance Scheme**).
2.  Answer the simple high-contrast YES/NO questions regarding land size, agricultural coordinates, registration cards, and crop types.
3.  The system calculates your eligibility status instantly.
4.  Read the detailed breakdown of documentation requirements and office filing steps provided beneath the result.

---

## 📍 Feature 3: Rythu Vedika Finder
Locate Rythu Vedika centers (common regional farmers' interaction halls built by the Government of Telangana) and get contact details for agricultural officers.

1.  **Filter by District**: Tap the district dropdown and select your local district (e.g., Nalgonda, Khammam, Warangal).
2.  **Mandal Coordinates**: Detailed cards will display the regional mandals, coordinator officer names, contact phone numbers, and physical office addresses.
3.  **Quick Call**: Tap the green phone icon on any contact card to make an immediate voice call to the designated agricultural officer for guidance.

---

## 📚 Feature 4: State G.O. Circular Library
Search and read official Government Orders (G.O.) and government circulars in a simplified, plain language format.

*   **Category Tabs**: Filter official notifications by **Subsidies**, **Pest Alerts**, **Seed Distribution**, or **Crop Loans**.
*   **Search Engine**: Type key search terms into the search bar to find matching files.
*   **Detailed View**: Click on any document card to open a full, readable layout containing the document summary, official number, release date, and key farmer-eligibility clauses.

---

## 🧮 Feature 5: NPK & Mandi Rates Calculator
A suite of tools to help you manage inputs, budgets, and track market sales:

### 1. NPK Fertilizer Calculator
Find the exact number of bags of Urea, DAP/SSP, and MOP required for your crop to save money on inputs:
*   Choose your crop type (Paddy, Cotton, Chilli, or Maize).
*   Enter your total farm acreage (e.g., 2.5 acres, 5 acres).
*   Tap "Calculate Requirements" to view the exact nutrient breakdowns and bag recommendations.

### 2. Live Mandi Crop Rates
View today's minimum and maximum market prices per quintal for major agricultural commodities in regional Telangana markets (e.g., Paddy, Cotton, Red Gram, Turmeric, Chilli).

### 3. Localized Crop Weather Advisory (PJTSAU Rules)
Select your district from the dropdown to access a real-time smart weather forecaster based on regional climate models:
*   Displays Air Temperature, Relative Humidity, Soil Moisture, Wind Speed, and Rain Probability.
*   Includes **PJTSAU (Prof. Jayashankar Telangana State Agricultural University)** smart directives (e.g., advice to delay chemical sprays during high winds, warnings to clear drainage canals during heavy rain, and evening watering instructions under hot weather).
*   Provides a 3-day agricultural trend outlook.

## 📱 Mobile Usability Advice

For the best experience when using Rythu Sethu in the field:
1.  **Direct Sunlight Reading**: Switch the text size to **Large** or **Extra Large** using the text scale toggle on the Chatbot screen.
2.  **Headphones**: Use headphones or turn up your volume to listen to the TTS "Speakout" read aloud when ambient field noises are high.
3.  **Offline Use**: Copy crucial fertilizer formulas, market prices, or phone numbers to your phone with the copy buttons before traveling to remote fields with weak cellular signals.
